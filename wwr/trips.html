Our Featured Rafting Adventures

Upper Colorado River

Rafting on the Upper Colorado River offers a serene yet invigorating experience, ideal for families, beginners, and nature lovers. Flowing through lush valleys and dramatic canyons near Glenwood Springs and Vail, this stretch features mostly Class I–II rapids with occasional Class III thrills during spring runoff. Rafters can expect gentle currents, crystal-clear water, and breathtaking views of red rock cliffs, cottonwood groves, and wildlife like bald eagles and bighorn sheep. It's a perfect introduction to white water rafting, blending adventure with tranquility in the heart of the Rockies.

Westwater Canyon

Rafting in Westwater Canyon delivers a thrilling blend of technical rapids and remote wilderness, making it one of the most sought-after whitewater experiences in the Southwest. Located near Moab, Utah, this stretch of the Colorado River features Class III and IV rapids that surge through narrow black granite gorges, creating dramatic drops and fast-moving chutes. Adventurers navigate legendary rapids like Funnel Falls, Skull Rapid, and Sock-It-To-Me, all framed by towering canyon walls and ancient rock formations. The trip offers both adrenaline and awe, with opportunities to spot wildlife and absorb the canyon’s stark, spiritual beauty.

Cataract Canyon

Rafting through Cataract Canyon is a breathtaking plunge into the heart of Canyonlands National Park, where the Colorado River unleashes some of its most powerful and unpredictable rapids. This stretch features over 25 named rapids, ranging from Class III to Class V during spring runoff, making it one of the most intense whitewater experiences in the West. Rafters navigate towering waves, deep chutes, and dramatic drops surrounded by red rock cliffs, ancient petroglyphs, and vast desert silence. Multi-day trips often include riverside camping, stargazing, and exploration of hidden side canyons, blending high adventure with spiritual solitude.

Grand Canyon

Rafting through the Grand Canyon is a once-in-a-lifetime journey that combines heart-pounding adventure with awe-inspiring natural beauty. As the Colorado River winds through nearly 280 miles of layered rock and ancient geology, rafters encounter legendary rapids like Lava Falls and Crystal, ranging from Class III to Class V depending on the season. Multi-day expeditions offer a deep immersion into the canyon’s grandeur, with side hikes to hidden waterfalls, starlit campsites, and moments of spiritual stillness amid towering cliffs. It’s a transformative experience where adrenaline meets reverence, and every bend reveals a new wonder.
